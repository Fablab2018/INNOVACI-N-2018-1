design Thiking

Our project is a 360 degree electrical outlet is ergonomic because it would occupy very little space and replace the old outlets, it is very attractive for its design and for the colors in which it can be presented, it also has secondary details that make it look like a device suitable for any type of environment, the security that provides an extension outlet as it is very unlikely that an accident can happen as it is of very little space

Project Management and Problem Statement

Our project is oriented to the human, we verify its viability, its functionality, we address a real issue and innovate to solve it.

Desing Thinking Workshop

We develop seed material where they explain the Design Thinking methodology.

Empathize

We use techniques to empathize, we refer and we inform all about our project, looking for a background of the problem to solve.

Define

We find the root cause of the problem and generate criteria to evaluate the information, we frame the problem in a direct approach, we define the insight of the user, client or consumer.

Devise

We use the Brainstorming technique to generate solutions, we use the technique of the 6 hats to find an integral solution evaluated from several points of view, we sketch the solution, where each detail of the sketch responds to a need.

Prototype and Test

We developed the functional decomposition of the solution describing it in a meticulous and detailed way, we developed a Gantt Diagram to execute the project implementation, we elaborated a list with the costs of the project, we developed an advertising campaign.
