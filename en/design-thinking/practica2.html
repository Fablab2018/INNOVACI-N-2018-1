Stages of Design Thinking

Design Thinking refers to a methodology for the generation of innovative ideas and that takes as a center the perspective of the end users. In this way, problems and needs can be detected, as well as offering effective solutions and, in many cases, alternatives for each of them.

Seed material

The questions arise from the definition of the problem or design principles as seeds for the ideas stage. Generates a seed large enough to have a large number of solutions and sufficiently limited or limited so that the team has certain limits that are helpful.
